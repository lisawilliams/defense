What should I do if I get a threat?

Nothing.

Don't just do something.
Sit there.

Once you've calmed down...

Talk to at least one other person.
Do nothing alone.

Threats are ambiguous: it's not always clear what's happening, or how to respond.

When are threats the most dangerous?

Escalate if:

  • Threats are specific: they name specific locations, people, actions.
  • They involve mob behavior online

What can I do proactively to stay safe?

Clean up your social media presence.

  • Scrubber
  • DeleteTweet

Take action to make yourself safe from doxxing.

Learn how with these great free courses and checklists from Trollbusters.

Take action to improve your security online. If you are not using a password manager...you should be.

  • LastPassword
  • 1Password
  • Turn on two-factor authentication
